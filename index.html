Hello, I'm Garrett Broberg and I'm a software developer in the Cleveland/Akron area. Initially I worked as a design engineer at a manufacturing facility in Brownsville, PA. After quitting that job in order to stay at home with my children for a few years, I returned to school to obtain a masters degree in 2017. Since moving to Cleveland I decided to pursue a career in software development. Recently, I completed the We Can Code IT boot camp in order to add fullstack web development to my skill set. I am fascinated by all things computer science, from data analytics, machine learning and natural language processing to databases and back/front-end development to cyber security. I enjoy learning new concepts and pushing myself to absorb new subjects and languages.
When my nose is not stuck in a computer or a book, I'm usually attending one of my sons' sporting events, fixing up my 1920's University Heights home, at work on the grill or in the kitchen.
