Hello, I'm Garrett Broberg and I'm a software developer in the Cleveland/Akron area. I have a Bachelors degree in mechanical engineering from West Virginia University and and Masters Degree in Data Analytics from Robert Morris University. Recently, I attended the We Can Code IT boot camp in order to add fullstack web development to my skill set. I enjoy back-end programming, machine learning and expanding my knowledge computer science concepts.

When my nose is not stuck in my computer I'm usually attending my son's sporting events, fixing up my 1920's University Heights home or cooking dinner.
